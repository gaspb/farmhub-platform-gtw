A flexible P2P and Serverless Web Services Solution
Modern Cloud technologies offer much more than the ability of replicating and scaling applications in localized private Datacenters.
Through the "Device-As-A-Host" approach of Cloud Hosting, we intend to let the community and IoT networks host themselves,
while getting most of the advantages default Cloud Solutions have to offer.
No data-center is needed when there are billions of connected devices with available resources around the world.

HighJack's Lab was built to let the end-user and the connected device manufacturers enter the web-hosting market share, creating the conditions of a self-endowing ecosystem ready for exponential loads

For the container user, it abstracts out the infrastructure layer to let architects and developers configure and deploy production-ready technological stacks, serverless functions and big-data processing pipelines, as-a-Service or to build upon
